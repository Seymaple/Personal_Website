About

My name's Sey Yeaple and I am a digital artist, that specializes in 3d work. I am a Penn State Graduate in Digital Art Media and Design (DAMD), formally known as IDS (Interdisciplinary Digital Studios). I work for the Office of Digital Learning (ODL) at Eberly College of Science at Penn State. I primarily work on creating 3D assets (modeling/texturing), 3D scans, animations, video compositing, VR and AR. I am proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and 3D programs including: Blender, Maya, Substance Painter, Unreal Engine, Marvelous Designer, Medium(VR), and 3D scanning (using Artec 3d).
